# watsonx.data Enablement Bootcamp

<!-- <img src="./assets1/class structure.png" width="100%"> -->

## Objectives
- Deepen the understanding of watsonx.data platform capabilities to articulate how the product capabilities can be used to solve our clients business problems.

- Develop mastery of the core technical skills of watsonx.data including data store(s), metastores, data ingestion and data curation (transformation, standardization and cleansing).

- Understand the importance of unifying the curated enterprise data assets to accelerate the deployment of AI and Analytical applications.

- Working and operating knowledge of the watsonx.data product that includes deployment options (SaaS, Hybrid and On-prem), configuring, managine access, designing data solutions and successfully implementing in a sandbox environment.

- Introductory understanding of the integration capabilities with watsonx.data Integration and watsonx.data Intelligence as well as popular third party technologies such as Snowflake and Databricks.

Additionally : Enhance the skills to handle the typical data challenges around 3Vs (volume, variety and veracity).  Deeper understanding of various methods and practices to successfully execute the Data Lakehouse, Data Unification,  Analytical and AI journey. 


## Bootcamp Structure
The bootcamp is structed to introduce IBM's Platform strategy, provide an overview of the Data Platform product portfolio, and deepen the understanding watsonx.data key components.  

The technical capabilites are introduced using a real business scenario that our Financial Services clients face today, and how IBM Data Platform capabilities could be used to overcome those challenges delivering business value to our clients. The bootcamp is structed based on an approach that demonstrates delivering incremental business value that could be applied in a client engagement.

The lab exercises will provide hands-on experience developing a solution for each of the four watsonx.data entry points. Completing all of the labs in this boot camp will help the watsonx practitioners to:
- Understand the power of watsonx.data to expose high quality data for Analytics and AI use cases.
- Gain hand-on experience in building an Agentic AI application.
- Articulate the importance of Information Architecture (IA) first approach
- Govern the data in the data lakehouse using IBM watsonx.data Intelligence
- Design data lakehouse for enterprises that can deliver incremental business value using IBM's data platform capabilties.

## Pre-work
In order to maximize the benefits of attending the bootcamp, the attendees should plan to complete the [IBM watsonx.data Technical Essentials Course](https://learn.ibm.com/course/view.php?id=16226) (3 hours).

For users who are new to databases, it is recommended that you complete the following **optional** learning activities as well: 


1. [What is a database (Blog)](https://www.ibm.com/think/topics/database)  (20 mins)
2. [SQL and Relational Databases Course](https://cognitiveclass.ai/courses/learn-sql-relational-databases) (3 hours).
